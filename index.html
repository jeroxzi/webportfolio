Hi, I'm Jeroxzi and I am a Full stack web developer

A Full stack web developer is responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining software applications. They work with programming languages and frameworks to create functional, efficient, and user-friendly software that meets the needs of businesses, organizations, and individuals. The Full stack web developer is involved in every stage of the development process, from analyzing requirements and designing software architecture to coding, testing, and deploying the software. They must also stay up-to-date with the latest developments in technology and programming languages to ensure that their software is innovative and efficient. In addition, a Full stack web developer must possess strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills to work effectively in a team and to communicate with stakeholders.
